About AIPalBot

We provide platforms for training numerous bots. Our trained bot is called Microbot and it could be trained by common users.

The only platform where humans and intelligent bots seamlessly integrate.

To have your own Microbot, go to aipalbot.com and sign up. Once you signed up, you will find instructions on how to train your own Artificial General Intelligence bot, which will be your digital copy on the internet.

We have provided the platform where you don't have to be a tech savvy to own a bot. You can train your bot anything, and you have options to make your trained instructions public or remained private within your profile account. If you have trained your Microbot some knowledge that can be useful to other people in the world, then publish such knowledge. Once you published, your Microbot will be available on our responsive intelligent search engine aipalbot.net We encourage all our users to train their Microbot useful knowledge and publish to the public. The platform is free, but for companies, institutions or individuals that need other specialize services, we have plans for different categories below.
